Explore a dystopian future where survival is a fight, power is a curse, and one woman must rise to become a legend. Books and Podcasts Coming Soon!

Book 1: Ember Rising

The Awakening Begins

In the neon-drenched streets of New Luma City, Fenix Rael’s life is forever changed when she stumbles upon The Orb, an alien energy source that fuses with her. Suddenly, she’s hearing whispers, unlocking impossible abilities, and becoming the target of OmniCorp—a ruthless megacorporation experimenting on humans. With the help of Jax, a cybernetically enhanced rogue, Fenix must outrun deadly hybrids, unravel a dark conspiracy, and decide whether to embrace or resist the power inside her.

But in a world where secrets are currency and trust is a luxury, survival may come at a cost she’s not ready to pay.

Book 2: Ember Ascended

The Fight Reaches the Stars

After barely escaping OmniCorp’s grasp, Fenix and her team launch an ambitious mission: infiltrate The Apex Station, a space-based facility controlling OmniCorp’s most dangerous experiments. As she battles both her enemies and the alien energy within, Fenix uncovers the horrifying truth—Project Nexus isn’t just about power, it’s about control.

With new allies and unexpected betrayals, the war escalates. As Fenix’s connection to The Core deepens, so does the risk of losing herself. Will she bring down OmniCorp from the inside, or will she become the very thing she’s fighting against?

Book 3: Ember Reborn

The Reckoning

The battle for freedom ignites as Fenix and her team take the fight directly to OmniCorp’s command center. But the war isn’t just about corporations anymore—something far older and more powerful lurks within The Core. As The Entity Protocol activates, unleashing the next evolution of hybrids, Fenix must face the ultimate choice: sacrifice her humanity or risk letting OmniCorp win.

With The Apex Station falling, Earth in chaos, and the past catching up to her, Fenix will rise—or burn.
