Support My Writing

Why I Need Your Support to Keep Writing

Hey there!

I’m pouring my heart and soul into creating immersive, thrilling, and emotionally rich stories—stories that pull you into new worlds, introduce you to unforgettable characters, and keep you turning the pages long past bedtime. Writing isn’t just a hobby for me—it’s my passion, my purpose, and something I want to keep doing for as long as I can.

My newest work, Ember Rising, is just the beginning of an epic journey. In this cyberpunk-inspired world, Fenix is thrown into chaos—haunted by alien whispers, hunted by powerful corporations, and forced to tap into a power she doesn’t fully understand. It’s a story about survival, identity, and the cost of wielding power. And trust me, things are only going to get more intense from here.

But here’s the thing—writing and publishing books, especially as an independent author, isn’t easy. It takes time, energy, and yes, money. That’s where you come in.

Why Your Support Matters

With your help, I can:

  • Continue writing and expanding Ember Rising into something truly epic.
  • Cover editing, professional cover design, and formatting to ensure the best reading experience.
  • Promote the book so it reaches the right audience.
  • Explore new formats like audiobooks or even graphic novel adaptations.

What’s In It for You?

Supporting me isn’t just about funding a book—it’s about being part of something bigger. As a sponsor, you’ll get:

  • Early access to new chapters and behind-the-scenes content.
  • Special acknowledgments—because I wouldn’t be here without you.
  • Exclusive goodies like signed copies, character art, and even the chance to inspire a character in the story.

Let’s Build This World Together

At the end of the day, stories thrive because of the people who believe in them. If you’ve enjoyed my work so far, or if you want to see more diverse, high-energy, and gripping stories come to life, I’d love your support.

Let’s make this happen together. Are you in?
